    Serious RP
    1. Main Rules
    - Do NOT RDM (Act of attacking or killing another friendly) -Can punch or slap, but if you are told to stop, stop. You CANNOT kill with - This is SeriousRP. That means you may not break character unless given permission to by a staff member - Sims MUST be held in designated Sim areas (Hangers, CSR, Citadel) - Do not asked to be whitelisted into jobs that you have not been promoted / trained for - Use common sense - Do not talk down on other battalions - Do NOT FailRP - Do NOT have your weapon out without permission - Cannot use Racism - PTS stand for Permission to Speak and it is not to be broken. -PTS is active in debriefs and promotion ceremonies -PTS can be called by the highest in the rank room, person hosting the meeting/sim, or gamemaster -If you are not one of these PTS applies to you -PTS is active for all in DB no matter the rank except for the leader of the DB. A high ranking player (CMD+, Commodore+ or Jedi Master+) can break PTS but only for a valid reason) - Regimental Commander+, Naval LTJG+, Jedi Advisor+ can call a promotion ceremony - Debriefs can happened after an event if the gamemaster chooses - Officers+, Shock Troopers, Naval, and Jedi Knights+ are allowed on third floor. Do not enter third floor without signing in. If you are not one of these roles listed you must be accompanied by one if not, you cannot enter (Unless ordered by one of the roles listed, Defcon 4 with battlestation, or Defcon 5). - Only Shock Troopers, Naval, and Engineers are allowed in the engine room. Do not enter the engine room without signing in. If you not one of these roles listed not you cannot enter the engine room at (Unless ordered by one of the roles listed, Defcon 4 with battlestation, or Defcon 5). - Do not enter another battalions barracks without permission from an officer or highest rank online from that battalion. - Do not have your weapons off-safety during Defcon 1 - All battalions must follow the Republic Hierarchy - Cannot destroy the tac inserts of other players -Can destroy tac inserts of event jobs if gamemaster allows it - Only CPT+, Shock Troopers, Naval LTJG+, Jedi Knight 8+ (VIII+), and Temple Guard can call AOS in advert with a reason stated -Calling AOS must be adverted - Everyone must be in TS to join a battalion. - Players can not switch jobs during an RP situation to escape that specific RP situation - Only Trainers and those with permission from Trainers can enter the CR room - Anyone can use the shooting range, but you MUST move for CR training. It doesn’t matter rank or how many people you have. - NO ONE can interfere with CR training (except TRI+) - If you were in an event. Debrief is mandatory. - You cannot leave debrief once you are in it unless you are: -dismissed -excused by the person running the DB, Regimental commander+, Rear Admiral+, or Jedi Master+ -training a CR -staff reasons
    2. Defcons
    - 1. Natural State (Weapons away, but can be holstered) - 2. High Alert (Weapons out and patrol the ship) - 3. All battalions search and clear the ship - 4. All battalions to battlestations - 5. All battalions defend 3rd floor and Engine room - 6. Evacuate the Ship -Defcons are to be called by the highest rank in there faction that is ONLINE.
    3. Claiming Rules
    - SMB+, Naval LTJG+, and Jedi Knight+ are allowed to claim any hanger, CSR, or Citadel -SGT+, Naval, and Jedi Padawan+ can claim Citadel - Must "/advert (Your battalion) claims (Location)" (exception is SGT+, Naval, and Jedi Padawan+ can claim Citadel using comms) - First person to advert gets the claim, rank does not overrule - Can only have a claim for a max of 4 hours (cooldown of 20 minutes before claiming again after the 4 hours, unless HA+ allows) - Must "/advert (Your battalion) unclaims (Location)" after use - If you do not leave a claimed area that is not yours the person with the claim may call and AOS if you do not leave after 3 vocal warnings (exception to the AOS rule) -If there is a person who is AFK ask staff to move them -Citadel cannot be used for tryouts - A battalion can only have one area claimed at a time - Regimental Commander can claim any hanger, CSR, or Citadel for themselves - Must “/advert I claim (Location)”
    4. Types of Chat
    /advert - RP Chat that can be used by only SMB+, Naval, and Jedi Knights+. -If comms are down NO ONE can use advert -Advert is used for announcements, orders, defcons, claiming areas, sims, or tryouts. -Advert can be used by event jobs if the gamemaster says you can, even while comms are down /comms - RP Chat that can be used by all players -If comms are down NO ONE can use comms -Comms is used for roleplay communications between forces, tryouts for lower than SMB, sims for Citadel only -Comms can be used by event jobs if the gamemaster says you can, even while comms are down /ooc - Out of Character is where you can talk about anything you wish that is not in character that can be seen across the whole server -Cannot disrespect players or battalions -Cannot use racism or discriminate -Event jobs cannot use OOC /looc - Local Out of Character is where you can talk about anything you wish that is not in character that can be seen in a shorter range -Cannot disrespect players or battalions -Cannot use racism or discriminate -Event jobs cannot use LOOC /y - Yell a roleplay chat which is the same as Say, but in a larger radius /w - Whisper a roleplay chat which is the came as say, but in a shorter radius /pm - Private message which is used to message someone in particular -Cannot disrespect players or battalions -Cannot use racism -Do not harrass players @ - Opens a staff ticket, but requires 10 characters minimum. Do not spam tickets.
    5. Cloaking Rules
    - Only RC can see cloaked and crouched enemies that are moving -RC can call out these enemies current location for others - All players can see standing and moving cloaked enemies - No one can see enemies that are completely unmoving and cloaked - If a cloaked enemy stop moving and you can no longer see them you may fire on their last known position - Use common sense. Doors don’t open by themselves. - If a cloaked enemy has a weapon out the weapon is cloaked as well, unless they are firing. - If the enemy on their own accord runs into you while cloaked and crouched you can see them. (You cannot purposely run into them or run in their path)
    6. Rebel Rules
    - Never break the brotherhood code (Never shoot another brother no matter the circumstances; except in a sim) - Do not go on the 3rd floor, Engine Room, or other Clones barracks without permission - Once you join a battalion / regiment. There is a 3-day cool down before you can leave it and join another one. Breaking this will result in a 1-week suspension from all battalions - Listen to higher-ups regardless of battalion in battle on/off ship. If not in battle this rule still applies, however, an exception while not in battle is if it interferes with inter-battalion business. - Must respect all higher ranks at all times - Promotions must be done in a public promotion ceremony or debrief. NO PRIVATE PROMOTIONS. - Must refer to all SGT+, Naval, and Jedi Padawan+ as "sir" - Must salute SMB+, Naval LTJG+, and Jedi Knight+ - Cannot engage a lightsaber user if a Jedi is engaging them
    7. Sith Rules
    Sith may not attack anyone at the Metorite Base unless Confirmed a Enemy Sith may not have Jedi Color Lightsabers, If do you get 1 warn than a job ban. Sith may attack the a person who is stalking them
    8. Jedi Rules
    - Battalion Jedi: Have only as much authority as the battalion commander gives them within that battalion. Jedi only have Military Control over the Jedi and Republic forces have control over the Clone Battalions. Only exception is Shaak Ti. - Jesus cross hilt, snake 5-blade and dani hilt are banned from all Jedi NO EXCEPTIONS!!!!!!! Pike type sabers are for Guardians, Masters, and Temple Guards only. - Padawans must use their assigned hilt. - Masters may only promote Knights twice per week. - No Jedi is allowed to use Red, Black, or Gray colors. - Jedi can’t use donator weapons on their jedi. - Do not use force lightning as any Jedi except Grey Jedi - Approved classes: Consular, Guardians, Sentinel, Temple Guard, Sage and Grey Jedi. -Cannot use Force Repulse outside of battle - Master's Abilities -Yoda: Promote and demote any Jedi and ban any jedi from a trial. -High-General: Holds as much power as Yoda. -Windu: Promote anyone to knight and demote knights, padawans, and younglings. Ban any jedi from a trial. -Named Masters: Promote knights to rank eight and demote knights, padawans, and younglings. If demoting a knight to padawan must tell Yoda and/or Windu why. Ban any jedi from a trial -Unnamed Masters: Promote to knight V. (Must stop at Knight V can’t go any higher) Can’t demote any ranks. (Unless someone breaks a major rule) -Generals: Demote and promote any ‘Rebel Military’ (not Jedi) (Excluding the high general)
    9. Rebellion Naval Rules
    All navals must be in communication with eachother - All naval must listen to ST orders - PO3 and up must know and understand both Medical & Engineer training programs. - CPO's Must have extensive training in both Medical and engineering - Only 1 Promotion per person every 2 days - Commodore+ can do a execution if necessary - No promoting for other battalions when on naval
    10. Donator Rules
    YOU MAY SWITCH TO THESE JOBS AT ANYTIME. Unless in a roleplay situation. - Jedi - Sith - Naval If a job is not listed here, do not switch to it, unlessed authorized - Do NOT switch to any "EVENT JOBS" when not in an event - Just because you are a donator, it does not mean you are immune to the rules. You will be treated, and dealt like any other player. - If you are trolling as an event job in an event you can be removed from the event and/or banned from being event jobs.
    11. Fail RP
    - Standing and sitting on players - Talking about Out of Character (OOC) things in RP - The only location where you can speak Out of Character (OOC) is in the OOC lounge or in TS (Unless designated as not OOC by the commander in that channel) - Do not salute/bow during combat situations, only salute on the Venator or at a Republic Forward Operation Base - No FailRP names - Can’t jump or sit in the moonpool - Can't be on top of Air Traffic Control (ATC) - You CANNOT sit or stand on the rail
    12. Staff Rules
    - Cannot break our server rules - Just because a ticket is made and you can’t handle it doesn’t mean you don’t take it. Take it and go up CoC - Do not disrespect players while in a sit (or anytime for that matter) - Do not take bribes - Do not go to a higher ranking staff member without permission - Do not use your staff abilities in RP situations - Do not spawn weapons or entities in RP ever. (Except for events by permission of Gamemaster) - Do not Administrate your own situation when you are RPing, contact another staff member to deal with the problem. (If no other staff is present and the player is repeatedly breaking rules, you can handle the situation.) - When you are staff you don't have "friends." Meaning you do not show favoritism toward your friends. (ex. Giving them ammo, failing to punish them for their misdeeds, defending a fail for your friends/faction etc.) - Do not whitelist yourself to jobs/roles in which you are not trained for or approved to use. (ex. Making yourself a commander when you are not) - Any staff caught on any other server breaking their rules or overall being unprofessional will be demoted. - No one shall staff more than one server, executives are exempt from this policy. - No staff can spawn themselves arrest batons to arrest or unarrest. The only time they can is when there are NO active ST on.
